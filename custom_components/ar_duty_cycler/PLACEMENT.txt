AR Duty Cycler — brand assets
=============================

OPTION A (easiest, Home Assistant 2026.3+):
  Drop the brand/ folder straight into the integration:

    custom_components/ar_duty_cycler/
    ├── __init__.py
    ├── manifest.json
    └── brand/
        ├── icon.png        (256x256)
        ├── icon@2x.png     (512x512)
        ├── logo.png        (820x256)
        └── logo@2x.png     (1640x512)

  Local images take priority over the brands CDN. No config needed.
  (Optional: add dark_icon.png / dark_logo.png for dark themes.)

OPTION B (brands repo PR, works on any version):
  Fork github.com/home-assistant/brands and add:

    custom_integrations/ar_duty_cycler/
    ├── icon.png
    ├── icon@2x.png
    ├── logo.png
    └── logo@2x.png

  Open a PR. Note: symlinks are NOT allowed in custom_integrations/.

source/ holds the editable SVGs and an alternate round (transparent-corner)
mark if you prefer that over the rounded-square tile.
